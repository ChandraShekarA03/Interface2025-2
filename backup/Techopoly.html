Non-Tech Event

Techopoly

Techopoly is a high-energy, life-sized board game inspired by monopoly, where strategy meets tech! Teams compete across tech-themed zones by solving coding problems, puzzles, and logic challenges. Buy properties, earn TechCoins, and dominate the board using wit, teamwork, and tech knowledge.

Event Format

  • Team Size: 3 participants
  • Duration: 120 minutes
  • No. of Teams: Max 8–9

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each team starts with 500 TechCoins
  • The board includes zones like Java Junction, Cybersecurity Circle, HTML Highway, etc.
  • Landing on a zone triggers a timed challenge (MCQ, puzzle, debugging)
  • Correct answer → buy the zone; Incorrect → move on
  • Rent applies if the zone is owned by another team
  • Special squares include Chance, Community Chest, and Math Jail
  • Teams earn coins by solving tasks and collecting rent
  • Completing a full board turn rewards +200 TechCoins
  • No phones or AI tools allowed; cheating leads to disqualification

Judging Criteria

  • Technical Accuracy – Correctness & clarity in solving challenges; grasp of core computer science concepts
  • Problem Solving – Logical thinking under pressure; performance in debugging/time-bound tasks; innovative thinking
  • Strategy & Resource Use – Smart spending & property acquisition; managing TechCoins and board control
  • Teamwork – Role division, communication, coordination

Winners will be decided based on final TechCoins, property value, and overall performance.
