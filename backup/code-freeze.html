Technical Event

Code Freeze: The Logic Lockdown

Code Freeze: The Logic Lockdown is a fast-paced, logic-centric coding escape room where precision thinking meets immersive storytelling. Participants enter a frozen code facility where they must "thaw" their way out by solving logic bugs, decoding traps, and debugging mysteries—no long code, just sharp minds and quick wit.

This isn’t your typical coding challenge—it’s a battle of logic and flow control, staged inside a single room transformed into a multi-zone escape chamber. Each round escalates in difficulty, with players physically advancing as they qualify.

Event Format

  • Team Size: 3 participants per team
  • Total Duration: 90–100 minutes
  • Organizing Team: 2 heads + 6–7 volunteers

Escape Room Setup

  • Each round is staged in zones within the same room
  • Teams physically move through zones as they progress

Rounds Overview

  • Round 1: Logic Drift (Easy) – 5 short puzzles, 10 min, 1 Time Freeze Token (+30s), Top 10 teams advance
  • Round 2: Frozen Flow (Moderate) – 4 debugging/dry-run tasks, 15 min, 1 Token (+45s), Top 7 teams advance
  • Round 3: Glacial Core (Hard) – 6 logic puzzles + 1 Frostbyte, 20 min, No Tokens, Top 5 teams advance
  • Round 4: Icebound Vault (Extreme) – 2 puzzles + 1 trap, 10–12 min, manual round, final winner revealed

Digital Integration

  • Google Forms (Rounds 1–3): Auto-grading + timers
  • Google Sites: Host rules and transitions
  • QR Code access between rounds (unlocked after solving)

Marking Scheme

  • Round 1: 5 Qs × 2 marks = 10
  • Round 2: 4 Qs × 3 marks = 12
  • Round 3: 6 Qs × 3 marks + 1 bonus = 15 + 10
  • Round 4: 3 Qs × 7 marks + 1 bonus = 21 + 10

Penalty System

  • Easy: Logic Slip –1
  • Moderate: Frostbite –2
  • Hard: Freeze Crack –3
  • Extreme: Ice Fault –5

Judging Criteria

  • Logical reasoning and debugging skill
  • Technical accuracy and flow handling
  • Final puzzle approach and clarity

Winning Strategy

  • Highest cumulative score across all rounds
  • Round 4 logic unlock + sealed winner reveal
  • Frostbyte bonus used as tie-breaker
