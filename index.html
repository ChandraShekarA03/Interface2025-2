About Us

Discover the legacy and vision behind INTERFACE 2025-2026

CHRIST University

Excellence in Education Since 1969

CHRIST (Deemed to be University) is a premier educational institution in Bangalore, known for its academic excellence and innovative approach to education. With a commitment to holistic development, the university has been at the forefront of technological advancement and research.

Department of Computer Science

Innovation Through Technology

The department fosters an environment of intellectual curiosity and practical learning. It hosts various national and international events to encourage student growth, with cutting-edge labs, experienced faculty, and active industry partnerships.

INTERFACE Fest Legacy

Celebrating Tech & Talent

INTERFACE has been a hallmark event for decades, showcasing student brilliance in IT and allied fields. From coding competitions to design challenges, it brings together talent from across India in a spirit of healthy competition and innovation.
